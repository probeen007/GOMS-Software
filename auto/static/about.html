OUR STORY

Built on Trust. Driven by Quality.

Established in 1978, P.M. Automobile Works has been serving Kathmandu with dedication and honesty. What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted automotive service center known for quality workmanship, genuine parts and excellent customer care.

Our goal is to keep your vehicle in the best condition and make every journey safe and worry-free.
