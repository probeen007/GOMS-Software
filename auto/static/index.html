Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my car? +
We recommend a general service every 6 months or 10,000 km, whichever comes first.
Do you use genuine parts? +
Yes, we always prioritize using OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) or high-quality aftermarket parts.
How long does a general service take? +
A standard general service typically takes 2-4 hours, depending on the vehicle's condition.
Do you provide warranty on repairs? +
Yes, we provide a warranty on both our labor and the parts we supply for your peace of mind.
Can I wait while my car is being serviced? +
Absolutely! We have a comfortable waiting lounge with complimentary Wi-Fi and refreshments.
