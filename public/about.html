Welcome to P.M. Automobile Works

P.M. Automobile Works was established in 1978 in Kathmandu, Nepal. With nearly five decades of experience, we have built a reputation for reliable, honest, and high-quality automobile services. Our skilled mechanics and technicians handle all kinds of vehicle repairs and maintenance.

We provide all kinds of automobile services including engine repair, electrical work, brake and suspension servicing, denting & painting, oil changes, and more. Delivering the highest degree of customer satisfaction through superior workmanship is our commitment.
