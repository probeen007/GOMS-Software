Role Access Summary

  • Admin: System control, staff directory, audit logs, financial reports.
  • Receptionist: Web booking verification, vehicle intake check-in, quotations, WhatsApp receipts.
  • Technician: Assigned workshop job cards, spare parts allocation, diagnostic work logs.
  • Accountant: Tax invoicing, payment recording, Day Book daily reconciliation, inventory purchase logs.

Operational Procedures

  1. Web Booking Verification: Incoming online service requests land in Front Desk queue. Staff verifies details and converts them to workshop appointments.
  2. Vehicle Check-In: Record physical vehicle arrival, odometer mileage, pre-existing damages, and condition photos.
  3. Quotation & Approval: Create itemized parts & labor estimate, change status to Sent, and share approval link with client.
  4. Workshop Execution: Technicians perform mechanical work, allocate parts from inventory, and log diagnostic findings.
  5. Invoicing & WhatsApp Receipt: Settle tax invoice, redeem loyalty points, and send WhatsApp receipt to client (+977).
  6. Daily Reconciliation: Accountants balance daily cash and bank balances in the Day Book.
